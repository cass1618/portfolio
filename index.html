Story (About Me)

I grew up in Sunriver, Oregon where I rode my bike, floated down the river, and learned to snowboard. After completing an AA degree at age 19 with a 4.2 GPA, I couldn't decide what to do with my life so I joined the Navy for 6 years. I studied nuclear power for 2 years in South Carolina and then worked in the nuclear plant aboard the USS Ronald Reagan aircraft carrier stationed out of San Diego. After finishing my contract I ended up living in Los Angeles for 5 years where I started bartending. I had a great time there but eventually realized that the Pacific Northwest is where I belong!
